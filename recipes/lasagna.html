Lasagna

asagna is a wide, flat sheet of pasta. Lasagna can refer to either the type of noodle or to the typical lasagna dish which is a dish made with several layers of lasagna sheets with sauce and other ingredients, such as meats and cheese, in between the lasagna noodles.

  • Species

Recipe

  2. Cook lasagna pasta
  3. Mix the mushrooms and ground meat then cook
